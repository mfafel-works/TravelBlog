Travel Tips

Plan Ahead

Book flights and accommodation early to save money. Done.

Pack Smart

Carry only essentials and keep copies of important documents. Don't forget to pack reef-safe sunscreen (others are illegal).

Stay Smiling

Wear a positive attitude and a smile for the best experience. You'll thank me later.

Stay Safe

Always be aware of local customs and safety guidelines. Don't touch the seals--they're endangered.
